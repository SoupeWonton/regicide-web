# Deck growth and grafts

Recruiting widens the deck; redundant exact kills deepen cards already owned.
Reinforcement is permanent and targets a card in hand:

- **Rank graft:** replace the target card's rank with the defeated enemy's rank.
- **Suit graft:** replace the target card's current suit with the defeated enemy's suit.

Example: exact-killing an already-owned `7♠` allows the player to choose one card in
hand and either replace its rank with `7` or replace its suit with `♠`. This is
replacement, never a flat `+1` and never an additional active suit.

There is no fragment currency or post-Council fragment shop. **Graft rearranging is the Sanctum's
verb** (2026-06-27); the **Forge forges spell crystals** ([`systems/items.md`](items.md)). Neither
mints new grafts — grafts come only from exact kills. Backfill may preserve campaign pacing, but it
must not become a second drafting economy.
