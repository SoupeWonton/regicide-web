# Items

Source decision: [`2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment.md`](../../../decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment.md).

## Equipment (five slots)

V3 power is carried in **five equipment slots, and only these five**:

- **Staff** — the **class ability**. Exactly one per character; everyone has a staff. It is the
  **swappable passive enabler** (it may read as an activated ability, but it is the passive
  signature layer, never a scaling resource). The class's **linear payoff ladder is separate and
  not swappable**.
- **Cloak · Ring · Hat · Amulet** — up to four **relics**. Relics are rare, run-defining
  rule-benders, now held as equipment in these slots rather than slotless. The earlier
  slotless-relic model is withdrawn.

Slot themes are intuitive placeholders only (Cloak ≈ roads, Ring ≈ economy, Hat ≈ recruitment,
Amulet ≈ activated) and are not yet fixed. The **Fallen Heroes** landmark lets the player swap the
staff, mixing one class's ladder with another class's staff. Exact relic content, slot themes, and
legal staff↔ladder pairings remain to be specified.

## Spells (crystals)

There are exactly **four spell identities — one per suit** (♣ attack · ♦ draw · ♠ block · ♥ recover),
held in a **gauntlet** (working name) that carries exactly four — one per suit. A spell is a scarce
one-use trump card in a dedicated area, not shuffled into the Tavern and not against the hand limit;
casting consumes it. At most one spell of each suit may be cast in a combat.

Each suit's spell is a **crystal** forged from suit-specific **fragments**:

1. **Fragment** — the floor; the suit's direct, castable emergency spell.
2. **Half** — forged from fragments; the **strongest castable** expression of the suit.
3. **Full** — forged further; **not castable.** It is set aside, permanently unavailable for the
   rest of the run, and serves as the **endgame / win token**. There is no castable third tier.

Forging consumes its inputs (fragments → Half → Full); the spell transforms, it does not accumulate
copies. Completing a crystal into a Full must itself be unlocked (endgame-gated). The current lean
is that spells sit **above suit immunity** (a ♦ spell is castable against a ♦-immune enemy), shown
explicitly. Exact spell effects, forge counts, and drop rates are to be specified.
