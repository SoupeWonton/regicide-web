# Classes

A class is a starting hand, a starting **suit path** (its home-suit linear ladder), and a separate
selectable **Staff** (a passive). The path and the Staff are **decoupled**. (Source decisions:
[`2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment.md`](../../../decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment.md),
clarified [`2026-06-27-v3.0-question-sweep.md`](../../../decisions/2026-06-27-v3.0-question-sweep.md).)

- **Each class has four suit paths** (♠/♥/♦/♣ ladders; content in
  [`facet-and-linear-candidates`](../../../wiki/v3/classes/facet-and-linear-candidates.md)). A class
  **starts on its home-suit path** — Sentinel ♠, Executioner ♣, Quartermaster ♦, Surgeon ♥.
- **The path is the payoff engine, kept not swapped.** In **V3.0 only the path's Continent-2 rung is
  live** (C3/C4/C5 rungs are unbuilt). The C2 rung is a **single ability — the ladder's first rung**:
  entering C2 grants it (revealed via an animation) and that is all; it does **not** rung up further
  within C2. **Completing C2 unlocks the other three paths**, which become all open and selectable on
  entering C2.
- **The Staff is a separate passive. Each class has four passive-signature Staffs** (the four
  candidates in [`facet-and-linear-candidates`](../../../wiki/v3/classes/facet-and-linear-candidates.md));
  on the **class-select screen at the start of C1 you pick one of your class's four**. It is never a
  scaling resource and not tied to the home suit. **Completing Continent 1 unlocks Staff-swapping at
  the Fallen Heroes shrine** (mid-C2); the swap offers **one randomly-drawn Staff from each of the
  four classes** (one per faction, drawn at random from that class's four — your own class included,
  so a swap may also re-roll within your class).
- Progression uses no separate point economy beyond this path + Staff + equipment model.

The initial roster is deliberately four sharply separated roles:

- Sentinel: block and defense (♠).
- Executioner: kill and aggression (♣).
- Quartermaster: combine and tempo (♦).
- Surgeon: persist and recovery (♥).

The kit content is **defined and locked** — 16 ladders (four suit paths per class) and 16 Staffs (four
passive signatures per class) — in
[`facet-and-linear-candidates`](../../../wiki/v3/classes/facet-and-linear-candidates.md); the six
formerly-provisional ladders (Bloodward, Harvest, Rationing, Munitions, Convalescence, Lifeline) are
**approved (2026-06-27)**. The enabler × payoff split is developed there; the
older linear and facet proposals live in
[`four-core-classes.md`](../../../proposals/classes/four-core-classes.md) and
[`facets-and-pressure-permutations.md`](../../../proposals/classes/facets-and-pressure-permutations.md).
The other five V2 classes are future unlock runway, not active V3 specifications.
