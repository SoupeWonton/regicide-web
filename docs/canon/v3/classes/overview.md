# Classes

A class is a starting hand, a **staff** (its innate ability), and a **linear payoff ladder**
keyed to its suit. (Source decision:
[`2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment.md`](../../../decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment.md).)

- The **staff is the swappable passive enabler** — the class ability, held in the Staff
  equipment slot; everyone has exactly one. It may read as an activated ability but is the
  passive signature layer, never a scaling resource.
- The **linear ladder is the payoff engine** and is **kept, not swapped.** A class starts with
  the ladder matching its own suit; the other suit ladders **unlock over the run**.
- Diversification comes from unlocking ladders and from **swapping staffs** at the
  **Fallen Heroes** landmark — mixing one class's ladder with another class's staff. Progression
  uses no separate point economy beyond this equipment model.

The initial roster is deliberately four sharply separated roles:

- Sentinel: block and defense (♠).
- Executioner: kill and aggression (♣).
- Quartermaster: combine and tempo (♦).
- Surgeon: persist and recovery (♥).

Exact kits and ladder content remain in design. The enabler × payoff split is developed in
[`facet-and-linear-candidates`](../../../wiki/v3/classes/facet-and-linear-candidates.md); the
older linear and facet proposals live in
[`four-core-classes.md`](../../../proposals/classes/four-core-classes.md) and
[`facets-and-pressure-permutations.md`](../../../proposals/classes/facets-and-pressure-permutations.md).
The other five V2 classes are future unlock runway, not active V3 specifications.
