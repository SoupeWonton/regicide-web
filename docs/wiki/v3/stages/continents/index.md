# Continents

**Summary:** V3 has **five continents, mapped 1:1 to the five pressure beats** (Claim→Master), with a God of Luck lore layer; their internal provinces, geography, and encounters are still undefined.

The expedition is five continuous beats themed as five continents: **C1 Claim · C2 Shape · C3 Exploit · C4 Adapt (the loop) · C5 Master (the opt-in God of Luck showdown)**. This is now canon ([[decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment|2026-06-24 decision]]); province content within each continent remains to be defined and must not silently import V2's structure. (sources: [[canon/v3/campaign/run-and-progression|Run and progression]], [[proposals/open-design-questions|Active design questions]])
