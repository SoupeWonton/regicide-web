# Executioner

**Summary:** Executioner is the proposed aggression class, defined by breaking exact-kill boundaries and converting precise kills into momentum.

The **Executioner** is Kingfall's aggressive, exact-kill character. Its confirmed identity is **Kill**: it should create or exploit kill opportunities, especially the precise kills that recruit an enemy or provide a replacement graft. The exact size of its kill window and every named ability remain unconfirmed proposals. (sources: [[canon/v3/classes/overview|Classes]], [[canon/v3/core-loop|Core loop]])

## What the Executioner is

An exact kill lands an enemy at exactly zero. If the card is unowned, it joins the expedition deck; if already owned, it supplies a rank-or-suit replacement. Executioner is intended to manipulate this progression-critical moment, making aggressive play feed future aggressive opportunities. Because exact kills change the deck, even a small window increase can create a campaign-wide snowball. (source: [[canon/v3/core-loop|Core loop]])

## Confirmed design

Executioner owns Kill and represents aggression. It is not canonically entitled to a `1–3 HP` kill window, free draw, royal recruitment, or cascading damage; those details belong to proposals written before every royal rule was settled. (sources: [[canon/v3/classes/overview|Classes]], [[canon/v3/campaign/structure|Campaign structure]])

## Linear progression proposal

The proposed start is `4♣`, `5♣`, and `2♣` with Edge and Undercut tools. **Open Season** widens exact-kill eligibility; **Kill Chain** draws after exact kills and grows momentum; **Reign of Terror** was written around recruited royal bomb-cards and therefore requires revision because current V3 treats royals as gate bosses rather than ordinary recruits. (sources: [[proposals/classes/four-core-classes|Four core classes]], [[canon/v3/campaign/structure|Campaign structure]])

## Facet proposal

| Facet | Proposed expression of Kill |
|---|---|
| **Momentum** | One exact kill accelerates the next. |
| **Bounty** | Improves pursuit of missed recruits and duplicate-graft targets. |
| **Kingslayer** | Converts road kills into preparation for royal gates. |

Momentum emphasizes immediate tempo, Bounty connects the character to [[v3/mechanics/deck-lifecycle|recruitment recovery]], and Kingslayer gives road precision a later payoff. Pressure can force the class to choose between survival now and preserving the next exact-value line. (source: [[proposals/classes/facets-and-pressure-permutations|Facets and pressures]])

## Not yet decided

- Starting hand and Act 1 kill loophole.
- Exact definition and numerical width of a kill window.
- Whether window changes apply to roads, gates, or both.
- Chain duration, draw timing, and reset conditions.
- Royal payoff after royal recruitment was removed.
- Linear versus facet progression and multiplayer ownership. (sources: [[proposals/open-design-questions|Active design questions]], [[proposals/classes/README|Class workspace]])

## Implementation status

The V3 Executioner kit is pending. Older simulations showed that an Executioner-like finisher increased exact kills without clearly increasing wins in base rules; that is historical evidence about one implementation, not validation of the V3 character. (sources: [[delivery/current-state|Current state]], [[research/simulation/harness-notes|Harness notes]])

## Connections

Executioner directly connects [[v3/mechanics/core-loop|combat damage]], [[v3/mechanics/conquest-first-acquisition|recruitment]], and [[v3/mechanics/replacement-grafts|card evolution]]. Its design must reward precision without making exactness automatic, and pressure must challenge its momentum without turning off the Kill station. (sources: [[canon/v3/core-loop|Core loop]], [[canon/principles/content-bar|Content bar]])
