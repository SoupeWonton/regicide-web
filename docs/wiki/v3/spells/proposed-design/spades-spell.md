# Spades spell — Guard Up / Brace

**Summary:** The proposed Spades spell is a consumable golden defense card that creates blocking access when the ordinary hand cannot provide it.

The proposed **Spades spell** supplies emergency defense when the hand cannot produce enough ordinary Spade value. It is a one-use golden card, distinct from [[v3/classes/sentinel|Sentinel's]] persistent block identity. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

| Crystal tier | Working identity | Castable? | Proposed effect                                                 |
| ------------ | ---------------- | --------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Fragment     | Guard Up         | Yes       | Reduce the current enemy's attack by a fixed amount.           |
| Half         | Brace            | Yes       | Convert one hand card into emergency Spade value during defense. |
| Full         | —                | **No**    | Not a spell: the retired win token. Forging it removes the Spades spell for the rest of the run. |

Brace (the Half) is intended to turn an otherwise unsuitable hand card into a defensive line. Whether its attack reduction persists, where the committed card goes, and whether it interacts with [[v3/relics/proposed-design/doorstop|Doorstop]] (now a relic equipment) are not settled. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

Bulwark Chant remains a liked V2-scale alternative, but the four-suit structure allows only one Spades identity. It may inform Guard Up's Fragment tier rather than become a fifth spell. (sources: [[archive/v2/items/items-v2|V2 item rebuild]], [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

## Authority and open questions

The crystal-spell model is the accepted V3 direction ([[decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment|decision]]). Fixed reduction, Brace semantics, immunity behavior, Doorstop interaction, and forge counts remain open. (sources: [[canon/v3/systems/items|Items]], [[decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment|Crystals decision]])
