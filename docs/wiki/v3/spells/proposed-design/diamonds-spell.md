# Diamonds spell — Quick Muster / Rally

**Summary:** The proposed Diamonds spell is a consumable golden draw card designed to rescue an inadequate hand before incoming pressure becomes lethal.

The proposed **Diamonds spell** is the emergency hand-access trump card. It exists for the moment when the player cannot reach ordinary Diamond draw and is approaching the game's common empty-hand failure state. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

| Crystal tier | Working identity | Castable? | Proposed effect                                                                                                |
| ------------ | ---------------- | --------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Fragment     | Quick Muster     | Yes       | Draw 2 cards immediately.                                                                                      |
| Half         | Rally            | Yes       | When a counterattack is declared, draw before defending — direction: "draw your counterattack / from discard"; formula and cap need rebalancing. |
| Full         | —                | **No**    | Not a spell: the retired win token. Forging it removes the Diamonds spell for the rest of the run.            |

Rally (the Half) must resolve before defense because drawing afterward cannot prevent an empty-hand death. The accepted direction ("draw your counterattack / draw from discard") is liked but needs rebalancing — an uncapped formula could turn a powerful royal into a free full hand; the cap and arithmetic remain open. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

The active lean allows the Diamond spell to work against a Diamond-immune enemy. Its suit identifies the axis it restores rather than subjecting it to ordinary immunity; this exception is the current lean, shown explicitly. (source: [[decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment|Crystals decision]])

## Authority and open questions

The crystal-spell model is the accepted V3 direction ([[decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment|decision]]). Rally's formula, draw cap, reaction timing, forge counts, and the immunity exception require rebalancing/consensus. (sources: [[canon/v3/systems/items|Items]], [[decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment|Crystals decision]])
