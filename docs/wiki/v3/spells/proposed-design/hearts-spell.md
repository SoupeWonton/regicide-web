# Hearts spell — Refit / Full Recycle

**Summary:** The proposed Hearts spell is a consumable golden recovery card that repairs both immediate hand access and longer-term Tavern circulation.

The proposed **Hearts spell** repairs expedition attrition. Unlike Tavern-order effects, it must improve immediate access as well as future circulation so that it matters while the engine is failing. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

| Crystal tier | Working identity   | Castable? | Proposed effect                                                                                        |
| ------------ | ------------------ | --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Fragment     | Refit              | Yes       | Return a small number of discarded cards to the Tavern; an immediate draw remains under consideration. |
| Half         | Recycle / Recover  | Yes       | Rebuild more of the Tavern from discard and provide immediate access.                                  |
| Full         | —                  | **No**    | Not a spell: the retired win token. Forging it removes the Hearts spell for the rest of the run.       |

Historical Refit returned three discarded cards, while the deeper recycle returned six and drew two. Their dual repair—future circulation plus present cards—is the reason the shape remains useful, although exact V3 numbers are not accepted. (source: [[archive/v2/items/items-v2|V2 item rebuild]])

The spell must also remain distinct from [[v3/classes/surgeon|Surgeon]], [[v3/landmarks/v2/camp|Camp]], and [[v3/relics/proposed-design/sainted-scalpel|Sainted Scalpel]] (now a relic equipment). A one-use rescue can overlap the Heart axis, but it must not make persistent recovery identity irrelevant. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

## Authority and open questions

The crystal-spell model is the accepted V3 direction ([[decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment|decision]]). Exact card counts, immediate draw, targeting, immunity behavior, and forge counts remain open. (sources: [[canon/v3/systems/items|Items]], [[decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment|Crystals decision]])
