# Clubs spell — Keen Edge / Commit

**Summary:** The proposed Clubs spell is a consumable golden attack card that develops from direct offense into a one-play combination rule break.

The proposed **Clubs spell** is Kingfall's one-use offensive trump card. It occupies the only Clubs spell position and is consumed when cast; holding it permits later upgrades instead of accumulating duplicate attack spells. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

| Crystal tier | Working identity | Castable? | Proposed effect                                                                                           |
| ------------ | ---------------- | --------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Fragment     | Keen Edge        | Yes       | Strengthen the current or next attack; exact value remains open.                                          |
| Half         | Commit           | Yes       | Add one extra hand card outside normal combination rules; choose its rank or suit contribution, not both. |
| Full         | —                | **No**    | Not a spell: the retired win token. Forging it removes the Clubs spell for the rest of the run.           |

Commit (the Half) is the most developed castable tier. Choosing rank supplies attack value; choosing suit can recover a missing suit axis without changing the attack total. That flexibility can preserve an exact kill while still producing a decisive exception to normal combination rules. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

The historical V2 Keen Edge doubled the next play, while Crownbreaker tripled it. Those values are references rather than accepted V3 text because raw multipliers may overwhelm exact-kill precision. (source: [[archive/v2/items/items-v2|V2 item rebuild]])

## Authority and open questions

The crystal-spell model is the accepted V3 direction ([[decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment|decision]]). Keen Edge's value, Commit's timing, immunity behavior, legal card contribution, and the forge counts behind each tier are undecided. (sources: [[canon/v3/systems/items|Items]], [[decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment|Crystals decision]])
