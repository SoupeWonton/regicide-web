# Clubs spell — Keen Edge / Commit

**Summary:** The proposed Clubs spell is a consumable golden attack card that develops from direct offense into a one-play combination rule break.

The proposed **Clubs spell** is Kingfall's one-use offensive trump card. It occupies the only Clubs spell position and is consumed when cast; holding it permits later upgrades instead of accumulating duplicate attack spells. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

| Tier | Working identity | Proposed effect |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | Keen Edge | Strengthen the current or next attack; exact value remains open. |
| Rare | Commit | Add one extra hand card outside normal combination rules; choose its rank or suit contribution, not both. |
| Mythic rare | Undecided | Must create a new trump-card decision, not merely a larger multiplier. |

Commit is the most developed tier. Choosing rank supplies attack value; choosing suit can recover a missing suit axis without changing the attack total. That flexibility can preserve an exact kill while still producing a decisive exception to normal combination rules. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

The historical V2 Keen Edge doubled the next play, while Crownbreaker tripled it. Those values are references rather than accepted V3 text because raw multipliers may overwhelm exact-kill precision. (source: [[archive/v2/items/items-v2|V2 item rebuild]])

## Authority and open questions

Canon currently removes standalone spells; this page documents an active proposal. Keen Edge's value, Commit's timing, immunity behavior, legal card contribution, and mythic tier are undecided. (sources: [[canon/v3/systems/items|Items]], [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])
