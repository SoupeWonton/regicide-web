# Hearts spell — Refit / Full Recycle

**Summary:** The proposed Hearts spell is a consumable golden recovery card that repairs both immediate hand access and longer-term Tavern circulation.

The proposed **Hearts spell** repairs expedition attrition. Unlike Tavern-order effects, it must improve immediate access as well as future circulation so that it matters while the engine is failing. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

| Tier | Working identity | Proposed effect |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | Refit | Return a small number of discarded cards to the Tavern; an immediate draw remains under consideration. |
| Rare | Full Recycle / Recover | Rebuild more of the Tavern from discard and provide immediate access. |
| Mythic rare | Undecided | Must provide an exceptional recovery without erasing expedition attrition. |

Historical Refit returned three discarded cards, while Full Recycle returned six and drew two. Their dual repair—future circulation plus present cards—is the reason the shape remains useful, although exact V3 numbers are not accepted. (source: [[archive/v2/items/items-v2|V2 item rebuild]])

The spell must also remain distinct from [[v3/classes/surgeon|Surgeon]], [[v3/landmarks/camp|Camp]], and [[v3/relics/sainted-scalpel|Sainted Scalpel]]. A one-use rescue can overlap the Heart axis, but it must not make persistent recovery identity irrelevant. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

## Authority and open questions

Canon still removes standalone spells. Exact card counts, immediate draw, targeting, immunity behavior, and mythic text remain open. (sources: [[canon/v3/systems/items|Items]], [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])
