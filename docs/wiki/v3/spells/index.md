# Spells

**Summary:** Four spell identities—one per suit—each a **crystal** forged from suit-specific fragments: **Fragment → Half** are castable (the two rungs), and a **Full** is the non-castable endgame/win token.

Spells are scarce one-use trump cards held in a **gauntlet** of four, not a broad inventory. A player can hold no more than one Clubs, Diamonds, Spades, and Hearts spell; casting consumes that card and there is no restoration. Forging all four crystals to Full strips the player's spells and is the opt-in path to the [[decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment|God of Luck showdown]].
