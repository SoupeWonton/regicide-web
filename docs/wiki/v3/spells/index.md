# Spells

**Summary:** The active proposal contains exactly four consumable golden spell cards—one per suit—which may upgrade vertically from standard to rare and possibly mythic rare.

Spell cards are proposed trump cards rather than a broad inventory. A player can hold no more than one Clubs, Diamonds, Spades, and Hearts spell; casting consumes that card and there is no restoration system. This proposal reopens current canon, which still removes standalone spells. (sources: [[canon/v3/systems/items|Items]], [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])
