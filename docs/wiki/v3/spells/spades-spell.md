# Spades spell — Guard Up / Brace

**Summary:** The proposed Spades spell is a consumable golden defense card that creates blocking access when the ordinary hand cannot provide it.

The proposed **Spades spell** supplies emergency defense when the hand cannot produce enough ordinary Spade value. It is a one-use golden card, distinct from [[v3/classes/sentinel|Sentinel's]] persistent block identity. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

| Tier | Working identity | Proposed effect |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | Guard Up | Reduce the current enemy's attack by a fixed amount. |
| Rare | Brace | Convert one hand card into emergency Spade value during defense. |
| Mythic rare | Undecided | Must create a singular defensive escape without becoming permanent immunity. |

Brace is intended to turn an otherwise unsuitable hand card into a defensive line. Whether its attack reduction persists, where the committed card goes, and whether it interacts with [[v3/relics/doorstop|Doorstop]] are not settled. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

Bulwark Chant remains a liked V2-scale alternative, but the four-card structure allows only one Spades identity. It may inform Guard Up's standard tier rather than become a fifth spell. (sources: [[archive/v2/items/items-v2|V2 item rebuild]], [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

## Authority and open questions

Canon still removes standalone spells. Fixed reduction, Brace semantics, immunity behavior, Doorstop interaction, and mythic text remain open. (sources: [[canon/v3/systems/items|Items]], [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])
