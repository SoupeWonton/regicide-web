# Diamonds spell — Quick Muster / Rally

**Summary:** The proposed Diamonds spell is a consumable golden draw card designed to rescue an inadequate hand before incoming pressure becomes lethal.

The proposed **Diamonds spell** is the emergency hand-access trump card. It exists for the moment when the player cannot reach ordinary Diamond draw and is approaching the game's common empty-hand failure state. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

| Tier | Working identity | Proposed effect |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | Quick Muster | Draw 2 cards immediately. |
| Rare | Rally | When a counterattack is declared, draw before defending; half the current attack with a cap is being explored. |
| Mythic rare | Undecided | A qualitatively stronger rescue, not unrestricted full-hand refill. |

Rally must resolve before defense because drawing afterward cannot prevent an empty-hand death. Letting incoming pressure determine draw creates the intended comeback, but an uncapped formula could turn a powerful royal into a free full hand; the cap and arithmetic remain open. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

The active lean allows the golden Diamond spell to work against a Diamond-immune enemy. Its suit identifies the axis it restores rather than subjecting it to ordinary immunity; this exception is not yet accepted canon. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

## Authority and open questions

Canon still removes standalone spells. Rally's formula, draw cap, reaction timing, mythic tier, and immunity exception require consensus. (sources: [[canon/v3/systems/items|Items]], [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])
