# Black Standard

**Summary:** Black Standard is a proposed recruitment relic that accelerates a conquered card into the next draw without granting it immediately.

**Black Standard** is a proposed recruitment relic that places a newly exact-killed recruit face-up on top of the Tavern. Its value is accelerated access, not information: the player must still produce a draw before using the conquered card. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

It is the fairer relative of [[v3/relics/crown-of-first-claim|Crown of First Claim]]. Crown creates immediate hand tempo; Black Standard creates a known setup opportunity that can be interrupted by the persistent fight state. The two may need mutually exclusive appearance rules because they solve the same recruitment-delay problem at different strengths. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

Exact insertion order, interaction with multi-card draw, full Tavern edge cases, and whether its lower power deserves scarce V3 relic space remain undecided. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])
