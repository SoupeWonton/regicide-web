# Hoard

**Summary:** Hoard is a historical V2 hand-cap relic retained as a V3 access benchmark and a test of overlap with Quartermaster identity.

**Hoard** is a developed V2 relic retained as a V3 comparison candidate. Its effect raises the holder's hand limit by 2, directly addressing the access and survival problem that often ends a run. (implementation: `server/campaign/content.ts`)

A larger hand creates more combination, exact-kill, and blocking possibilities without introducing a new currency. Its reliability also makes it a useful baseline: a more thematic relic must produce enough practical advantage to compete with two additional card positions. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

Hoard is not accepted V3 content. It may overlap strongly with [[v3/classes/quartermaster|Quartermaster]], and its balance depends on multiplayer hand limits, Diamond draw, overdraw rules, and whether class bonuses stack. (sources: [[canon/v3/systems/items|Items]], [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])
