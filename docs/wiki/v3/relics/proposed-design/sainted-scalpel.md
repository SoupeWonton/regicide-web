# Amulet Of Renewal

**Summary:** Sainted Scalpel is a historical V2 recovery relic retained as a V3 benchmark for effects that repair circulation and immediate access together.

**Sainted Scalpel** is a V2 recovery relic retained as a V3 comparison candidate because it repairs both future circulation and the current hand. It is not accepted V3 content. (sources: [[archive/v2/items/relics|V2 relics]], [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

V2 sources contain different tunings: the later design rebuild says shuffle up to 4 discarded cards into the Tavern and draw 1, while the implemented ascending-deck catalog used a stronger shuffle-up-to-6 and draw-2 version. The V3 brainstorm preserves the dual-purpose shape, not either number as canon. (sources: [[archive/v2/items/items-v2|V2 item rebuild]], implementation: `server/campaign/content.ts`)

Its main design question is class overlap. A rare relic may excitingly amplify [[v3/classes/surgeon|Surgeon]] and give other classes access to recovery, or it may erase the distinction between a one-off relic engine and the class's persistent identity. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])
