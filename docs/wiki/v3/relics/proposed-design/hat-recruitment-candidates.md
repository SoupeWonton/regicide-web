# Hat relics — recruitment *(proposed candidates)*

**Slot theme (locked 2026-06-28):** **Hat = recruitment** — exact-kill → recruit. The hazard is
**Executioner** (recruit-to-hand, graft-any). So Hat candidates bend recruitment rules the class
*doesn't*: **what** is recruitable, recruit-on-overkill, where recruits go, and recruit quality.
(source: [[proposals/systems/relic-candidates-by-slot|Relic candidates by slot]])

**Review pass 1 (Landry, 2026-06-28):** keepers refined; immunity-steal and unclear entries cut; pool
**expanded** (this slot was too thin). **Priority slot for further expansion.**

> **✅ Officialized 2026-06-28 → [[canon/v3/systems/relics|`relic_v1_design_3.0`]].** In the pool:
> Conscription · Press-gang · Rallying Cry · Battlefield Promotion · Black Standard · Apprentice ·
> Muster · Plunder. **Held** (still `proposed`): **Spoils** (maybe too strong — narrow first).

**Legend:** ⚠ maybe too strong — narrow it · ✦ from this pass · ★ a reworked classic.

## Active candidates (reviewed)

| Relic | The one rule it bends | Status / note |
|---|---|---|
| **Conscription** | An overkill still recruits the card, but it enters carrying a **−1 token** (instead of being lost to discard). | ✓ liked; the −1 token is the cost. |
| **Press-gang** | Cards you recruit arrive **pre-shaped** — choose rank-or-suit as if grafted — instead of as printed. | ✓✓ very liked. |
| **Rallying Cry** | When you recruit, also return one card from discard to the Tavern (the recruit rallies a fallen one). | ✓ liked. |
| **Battlefield Promotion** | The first card you recruit each fight enters upgraded one rank. | ✓ liked. |
| **Spoils** | When you exact-kill, you also recruit one card you previously skipped on this road. | ⚠ maybe too strong — narrow (e.g. once per road, skipped-only). |
| **Black Standard** ★ | A recruited card enters the **top of the Tavern** (your next draw) instead of the bottom. | ✦ the *Hat of Familiarity* classic, reworked in; fairer than recruit-to-hand. |
| **Apprentice** | You may immediately discard a fresh recruit to **draw 2** (cash a spoil you don't want). | ✦ recruit → cards option. |
| **Muster** | Recruited **royals** (J/Q/K) enter the Tavern top instead of the bottom — faster access to recruited bombs. | ✦ tamer than the cut *Triumph*. |
| **Plunder** | On an exact kill, you may swap the recruit for a **same-suit card already in your discard**. | ✦ choose *what* you recruit. |

## Cut

- **Triumph** — royal recruits to hand at full value. **Way too strong.** (Tamer version survives as *Muster*.)
- **Usurper** & **Mockery** — "take the enemy's immunity" on recruit. **Confusing** — what immunity-steal
  means wasn't legible. Cut from Hat. *(If the immunity thread is wanted, it lives better on the Amulet
  [[v3/relics/proposed-design/amulet-activated-candidates|Unbinding]].)*
- **Annex** (buy a recruit without killing) & **Double Draft** (recruit two on a kill) — **unclear /
  to be reworked.**

## Open for the rework

This slot is the **priority for more candidates**. Recruitment rules still untapped: *what tier* of
card is recruitable, recruiting from the discard, recruit-and-graft combos, and recruit *placement*.
