# Warhorn

**Summary:** Warhorn is a historical V2 damage relic retained as a V3 benchmark because its reliable bonus also reshapes exact-kill arithmetic.

**Warhorn** is a V2 relic retained as a V3 comparison candidate. Its developed effect adds 2 damage to every play, making its contribution immediate and unmistakable. (sources: [[archive/v2/economy-and-identity|V2 economy and identity]], implementation: `server/campaign/content.ts`)

Although numerically simple, universal +2 is not pure upside in an exact-kill game: it accelerates ordinary defeats while changing every recruitment calculation and creating overkill risk. That tension may make Warhorn a useful benchmark against more elaborate identity relics. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

Warhorn is not accepted V3 content. Whether its reliable frequency overwhelms rarer conditional relics, how it interacts with Clubs and multipliers, and whether it produces meaningful adaptation rather than rote arithmetic require testing. (sources: [[canon/v3/systems/items|Items]], [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])
