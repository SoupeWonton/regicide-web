# Ring relics — economy *(proposed candidates)*

**Slot theme (locked 2026-06-28):** **Ring = economy** — cards-as-resource. Hazard: **Quartermaster**
(flat hand+, combo→draw, pay-double, search). Guardrails from review: **no alternative economy, no
stacking, no new wallet.** (source: [[proposals/systems/relic-candidates-by-slot|Relic candidates by slot]])

**Review pass 1 (Landry, 2026-06-28):** keepers refined; *Two-Face / Avarice / Vault / Bottomless Purse*
cut; pool topped up. **A bit more still wanted here.**

> **✅ Officialized 2026-06-28 → [[canon/v3/systems/relics|`relic_v1_design_3.0`]].** In the pool:
> Hoard · Interest · Debt · Requisition Writ · Liquidate · Last Coin · Caravan Coin · Double or Nothing.
> **Held:** **Prospector** — deferred to the **fragments** decision (it grants a fragment).

**Legend:** 🔁 brushes a class lane · 🩹 floor-relief · ★ kept reference · ✦ from this pass.

## Active candidates (reviewed)

| Relic | The one rule it bends | Status / note |
|---|---|---|
| **Hoard** ★ | Your maximum hand size is +2. | ✓ kept; the power benchmark. |
| **Interest** | If you discarded nothing to the previous enemy, start the next fight +1 card. | ✓ liked. |
| **Debt** | Once per fight, draw 2 now, then discard 1 at the start of each of your next two turns. | ✓ liked. *(Could also be an [[v3/relics/proposed-design/amulet-activated-candidates|Amulet]].)* |
| **Requisition Writ** | Once per landmark, convert **2 cards** of hand value into one fragment of your choice. | ✓ lightened 5 → 2. |
| **Liquidate** | Once per fight, discard one card to draw 2 (net +1 card). | ✓ liked. |
| **Last Coin** | **Once per fight, the first time you begin a turn empty-handed, draw 3.** | 🩹 reworked to begin-of-turn, once per fight. |
| **Caravan Coin** | Your pay-from-hand cost at the Caravan / Market is reduced by 2 cards' value. | ✦ landmark economy; no new currency. |
| **Prospector** | Clearing a road grants 1 fragment of your choice. | ✦ feeds the *existing* fragment economy — confirm it's not over the no-wallet line. |
| **Double or Nothing** | Once per fight, discard your whole hand and draw that many cards **+1**. | ✦ risk/reward refresh; one-shot, no stacking. |

## Cut

- **Two-Face Ring** — Ace as shield. Unclear / disliked.
- **Avarice** — overdraw bank. **Effectively a duplicate of Hoard.**
- **The Vault** & **Bottomless Purse** — banking a card / auto-reshuffle. **Alternative economy** — out.
