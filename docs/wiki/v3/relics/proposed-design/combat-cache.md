# Amulet of Alchemy

**Summary:** Combat Cache is a historical V2 combination relic retained as a V3 benchmark and a test of overlap with Quartermaster identity.

**Combat Cache** is a V2 relic being retained as a V3 power benchmark. Its implemented effect allows legal matched-card combinations to total 12 instead of 10, immediately changing which plays are possible. (sources: [[archive/v2/items/relics|V2 relics]], implementation: `server/campaign/content.ts`)

The effect was selected and understood because it changes access rather than requiring a new meter. Its V3 problem is overlap with [[v3/classes/quartermaster|Quartermaster]], whose proposed identity already breaks combination limits. Testing must determine whether the relic provides healthy cross-class access, exciting Quartermaster amplification, or simple identity theft. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

Combat Cache is not accepted V3 content. Its tier, stacking rule, interaction with Aces and grafted ranks, and final combination ceiling remain open. (sources: [[canon/v3/systems/items|Items]], [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])
