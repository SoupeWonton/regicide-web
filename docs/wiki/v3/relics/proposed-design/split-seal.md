# Split Seal

**Summary:** Split Seal is the leading V3 relic candidate for deliberately targeting replacement grafts anywhere in the owned expedition deck.

**Split Seal** is the leading proposed V3 relic for deliberate deck shaping. After an exact kill against an already-owned card, its rank-or-suit replacement may target any owned card in the hand, Tavern, or discard instead of only a card currently held. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

The relic changes target access without changing graft power: the defeated card still supplies exactly one replacement rank or suit. It lets the player pursue rank concentration, suit concentration, class synergy, or preparation for later pressure rather than accepting whichever legal target happened to be drawn. (sources: [[canon/v3/systems/deck-and-grafts|Deck and grafts]], [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

Split Seal is proposed, not canon. Search UI, no-op targets, card-history display, multiplayer ownership, and whether unrestricted access is too strong remain unresolved. (sources: [[canon/v3/systems/items|Items]], [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])
