# Crown of First Claim

**Summary:** Crown of First Claim is a deliberately powerful recruitment relic candidate that puts a newly conquered card directly into the hand.

**Crown of First Claim** is a proposed high-power relic that places a newly exact-killed recruit directly into the player's hand. It converts conquest into immediate combat access rather than waiting for ordinary Tavern circulation. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

The proposal intentionally tests the exciting version before weakening it. Across many encounters, repeated immediate recruits may create a powerful tempo engine; that possibility is part of the playtest question rather than a reason to pre-emptively remove the effect. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])

Frequency limits, full-hand behavior, multiplayer ownership, and interactions with exact-kill classes remain undecided. It is stronger than [[v3/relics/black-standard|Black Standard]], which accelerates the recruit without putting it directly in hand. (source: [[proposals/systems/relics-and-spell-cards|Relics and spell cards proposal]])
