# Relics

**Summary:** V3 relics are rare, run-defining rule-benders held as **equipment** in four slots — **Cloak · Ring · Hat · Amulet** — alongside the **Staff** (the class ability). The slotless model is superseded; the exact catalog and slot themes remain under design. (Source: [[decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment|crystals, continents & equipment decision]].)

The candidate pool below is re-standardized into the equipment slots. Slot themes (Cloak ≈ roads, Ring ≈ economy, Hat ≈ recruitment, Amulet ≈ activated) are intuitive placeholders, not fixed. Staffs are swapped at the **Fallen Heroes** landmark. A reference page does not make its historical behavior V3 canon. (sources: [[canon/v3/systems/items|Items]], [[decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment|Crystals decision]])
