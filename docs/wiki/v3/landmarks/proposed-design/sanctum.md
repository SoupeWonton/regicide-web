# Sanctum — *Attune* ⚠️ role superseded

> **⚠️ Superseded by the 2026-06-24 crystal model.** Spells are no longer *attuned* (awaken /
> silver→gold→purple). They are **forged from suit-specific fragments at the [[v3/landmarks/proposed-design/forge|Forge]]**
> (Fragment → Half → Full). The "Attune a spell" verb below no longer has a system to serve.
> **Open question:** does Sanctum get a new verb (e.g. a recovery/blessing rite, or a fragment
> source) or is it removed? See [[decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment|the decision]].

## Original proposed design (historical — the verb is now obsolete)

One verb: **Attune** — acquire or upgrade **one** of the four suit spell-cards (silver → gold →
purple). This depended on the superseded vertical-tier spell model; the crystal model moves spell
assembly to the Forge.

## If Sanctum survives, to design
- [ ] A new one-verb decision that doesn't duplicate the Forge (crystals), Camp (rest), or Lair (relics).
- [ ] Whether it instead becomes a **fragment source** or a recovery/consecrate rite.
