# Camp — *Prepare*

**Proposed design (fresh).** One verb: **Prepare.** See
[[v3/landmarks/proposed-design/philosophy-and-design-orientation|philosophy & orientation]].

## The one decision
> Which prep axis do you take into the next fight — and is the rest worth the stop?

## What it does
Four-axis preparation for the next encounter, one per suit:
- **♦** redraw your hand
- **♥** reshuffle the discard into the Tavern
- **♠** start the next fight with a shield
- **♣** your first play deals double

## Why route here / opportunity cost
Camp buys *consistency* rather than power. The stop itself is the cost (a fight's worth of route
not spent acquiring). **Camp must not be mandatory** — if every run must rest here, it's a tax, not
a choice.

- [x] Guaranteed rest cadence vs Camp-as-optional (ties to D12 rest cadence).
