# Hunt — *Pursue*

**Proposed design (fresh).** One verb: **Pursue.** See
[[v3/landmarks/proposed-design/philosophy-and-design-orientation|philosophy & orientation]].

## The one decision
> Which **missed or unowned recruit** do you chase — accepting you still have to fight and exact-kill it?

## What it does
Targets a specific recruit you didn't get. It does **not** hand you the card — it puts that enemy in
front of you so you can **exact-kill** it under your own power. Turns route luck into **authored
recruitment** while respecting conquest-first acquisition.

## Why route here / opportunity cost
The cost is a **fight you choose to take** plus the route stop. You're spending effort to fix a gap
in the deck instead of acquiring something new.
