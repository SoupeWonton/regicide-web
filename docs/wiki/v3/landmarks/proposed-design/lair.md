# Lair — *Raid*

**Proposed design (fresh).** One verb: **Raid.** See
[[v3/landmarks/proposed-design/philosophy-and-design-orientation|philosophy & orientation]].

## The one decision
> Do you risk a dangerous fight for the **advertised relic** — knowing retreat or defeat grants nothing?

## What it does
The **primary relic source**. The relic (or its family) is advertised *before* commitment;
**victory grants it**, retreat or defeat does not. A relic is a rare, rule-bending exception held as
**equipment** in one of the four relic slots (Cloak/Ring/Hat/Amulet) — see the
[[decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment|2026-06-24 decision]].

## Why route here / opportunity cost
The cost is the **fight itself** — the cards spent and the risk of entering the next road battered.
Routing to a Lair surrenders a Caravan/Forge stop and gambles resources on a single reward.
