# Caravan — *Pay-from-hand*

**Proposed design.** One verb: **Pay-from-hand.** See
[[v3/landmarks/proposed-design/philosophy-and-design-orientation|philosophy & orientation]].

## The one decision
> Is a **relic (equipment)** worth **entering the road with fewer cards** to pay for it?

## What it does
A costly **relic** bought with a **visible discard-total cost** — *no gold, no spendable currency*
(canon: no secondary wallet). You pay by surrendering cards from your deck/hand, then continue the
road lighter. **Spells are no longer sold here** — they are forged from combat-dropped fragments at
the [[v3/landmarks/proposed-design/forge|Forge]]. (The spell-crystal *fragments* are not a wallet
and cannot be spent at the Caravan.)

## Why route here / opportunity cost
The cost is **literal card economy** — the thing that keeps you alive. A Caravan is the *expensive*
alternative to a Lair (relic earned by fight): same kind of reward, paid in attrition.
