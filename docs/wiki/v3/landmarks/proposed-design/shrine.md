# Shrine — *Consecrate*

**Proposed design (fresh).** One verb: **Consecrate** — the newest landmark (resolved 2026-06-22).
See [[v3/landmarks/proposed-design/philosophy-and-design-orientation|philosophy & orientation]].

## The one decision
> Which owned card do you **permanently transmute** — its suit (or rank) into a chosen value — without a kill?

## What it does
Permanently **transmutes one owned card's suit (or rank)** into a chosen value, **no kill required**.
One card, one sharp choice per visit. It *converts*, never thins — deck size is unchanged
(respects no-removal).

## Why route here / opportunity cost
Consecrate is the **no-kill answer to a denied axis** — "I keep drawing the wrong suit and can't
exact-kill the one I need." It's *player-authored* reshape, paid by routing here and skipping a
fight/relic/spell stop, so it eases the floor without becoming a card faucet.
