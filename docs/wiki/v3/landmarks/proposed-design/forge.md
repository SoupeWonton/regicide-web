# Forge — *Rearrange (grafts) & assemble (crystals)*

**Proposed design.** Verbs: **Rearrange** earned grafts, and **assemble** spell crystals. See
[[v3/landmarks/proposed-design/philosophy-and-design-orientation|philosophy & orientation]].

## The one decision
> Which earned graft do you move (to re-aim your deck) — and/or do you forge collected fragments into a Half, or a (run-ending) Full?

## What it does
**Grafts:** moves the rank/suit grafts you've already earned by conquest; **never grants new graft
power**, preserving conquest permanence.
**Crystals (2026-06-24):** assembles suit-specific **fragments → Half**, and — once unlocked —
**Half/fragments → Full** (a Full is non-castable, retiring that spell for the endgame). This is the
home for the [[v3/mechanics/items-and-power-vehicles|crystal spell economy]]. It still **never hands
out relics**. (Earlier "never hands out spells" no longer holds — the Forge *forges* them from
fragments; it does not gift finished spells.)

## Why route here / opportunity cost
Forge is pure **re-aiming** — it turns a graft that landed on the wrong card into one that fits the
plan. The cost is the stop and the irreversibility of committing a graft elsewhere.
