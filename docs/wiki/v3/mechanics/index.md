# Mechanics

**Summary:** V3 mechanics explain how cards move through draw, combination, killing, blocking, persistence, conquest, and permanent transformation.

The mechanical center is the shared card resource. Players draw, combine cards, pursue exact kills, survive counterattacks, and carry the resulting hand, Tavern, discard, recruits, and grafts forward through the expedition.
