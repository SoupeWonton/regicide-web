# Items and power vehicles

**Summary:** V3 power lives in the conquered deck, **five equipment slots** (Staff + Cloak/Ring/Hat/Amulet), and **four crystal spells** in a gauntlet. (Source: [[decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment|crystals, continents & equipment decision]].)

Kingfall concentrates lasting power in the conquered deck and replacement grafts. On top of that sit two power systems: **equipment** (the class ability as a Staff, plus up to four relics) and **spells** (four suit crystals). The earlier slotless-relic and spell-removal canon is superseded by the 2026-06-24 decision. (sources: [[canon/v3/systems/items|Items]], [[decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment|Crystals decision]])

## Equipment (five slots)

V3 has five equipment slots, **and only these five**:

- **Staff** — the class ability. Exactly one per character. It is the **swappable passive enabler** (the class's payoff **ladder stays separate and is not swapped**). Swapped at the **Fallen Heroes** landmark, mixing one class's ladder with another's staff. (see [[v3/classes/facet-and-linear-candidates|enabler × ladder]])
- **Cloak · Ring · Hat · Amulet** — up to four **relics**, now held as equipment rather than slotless. Slot themes are intuitive placeholders only (Cloak ≈ roads, Ring ≈ economy, Hat ≈ recruitment, Amulet ≈ activated), open to brainstorming.

Relics remain rare, run-defining rule-benders that must change an attributable decision, not provide invisible decoration. (sources: [[canon/v3/systems/items|Items]], [[canon/principles/content-bar|Content bar]])

## Crystal spells

There are exactly four spell identities: [[v3/spells/proposed-design/clubs-spell|♣ Clubs]], [[v3/spells/proposed-design/diamonds-spell|♦ Diamonds]], [[v3/spells/proposed-design/spades-spell|♠ Spades]], and [[v3/spells/proposed-design/hearts-spell|♥ Hearts]], held in a **gauntlet** of four (one per suit). A player may hold at most one per suit; casting consumes it; at most one of each suit may be cast in a combat. There is no recharge or restoration.

Each spell is a **crystal** forged from suit-specific **fragments**:

- **Fragment** — the floor, castable.
- **Half** — forged from fragments; the strongest castable expression.
- **Full** — forged further; **not castable**, set aside permanently, the endgame/win token. Forging all four to Full strips the player's spells and opens the [[decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment|God of Luck showdown]].

Suit communicates the emergency axis. The current lean lets spells operate **above matching enemy immunity** (else the emergency Diamond crystal would fail against the enemy producing the draw crisis it answers), shown explicitly. Holding fragments is also a forging decision: spend now to solve the fight, or hoard toward a Half — or toward the Full that retires the spell for the endgame.

## Forging & acquisition

Fragments drop in combat (≈1 in 4 combats yields fragments). The **Forge** assembles them (fragments → Half → Full); completing a Full must itself be unlocked. Forge counts and drop rates are open. The [[v3/landmarks/v2/market|Caravan]] and [[v3/landmarks/v2/lair|Lair]] remain candidate sources for relics; pay-from-hand pricing is still under discussion.

## Not yet decided

- Exact Fragment and Half spell text and numbers (incl. the Diamonds draw direction).
- Forge counts (fragments → Half ≈ 2; → Full "a lot") and drop rates.
- Whether spells formally override matching immunity (current lean: yes).
- Slot themes for Cloak / Ring / Hat / Amulet, and which existing relic candidate lands where.
- The gauntlet's final name and any "all four held" behavior.
- Legal staff↔ladder pairings at Fallen Heroes; relic acquisition pricing. (source: [[decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment|Crystals decision]])
