# Surgeon

## Identity

Tier: 1 — Core Hero
Theme: Recovery / Precision
Suit: Hearts
Guiding question: How do we recover mistakes?

The Surgeon is the expedition's survival recycler. This class converts Hearts from soft utility into hard campaign endurance and death mitigation.

## Expedition Stability Profile

| Pillar | Rating | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Shield | 0 | — |
| Access | 0 | — |
| Recovery | 3 | primary function |
| Initiative | 0 | — |
| Consistency | 1 | deck recycling reduces long-run entropy |

CT per chapter: ~0.75 to ~1.0
Specialization bonus: Physician +0.5 to +0.75 / Alchemist +0.25 to +0.5
Primary failure mode addressed: discard check failure and boss transition collapse

Core role:
- restore long-run card economy
- reduce collapse from cascading discard checks
- keep wounded expeditions alive through bad variance

## Core Ability

The first Heart trigger each enemy recovers +1 additional card.

### What it actually does

- increases effective deck lifespan in continuous road segments
- raises value of medium Hearts in attrition-heavy chapters
- allows more aggressive short-term plays because recovery is stronger

### Timing window

Best use cases:
- when discard pile has high-value recoverables
- before expected long encounter chains
- after a costly counterattack cycle

Worst use cases:
- with low-quality discard pile (recovering junk)
- when immediate lethal is required and recovery has no time to matter

## Specialization Paths

## Physician — Recovery

When a hero would die, once per chapter prevent that death. The hero immediately discards their entire hand and draws 1 card.

### Effect profile

- the only class ability that directly prevents death
- the cost is severe: an empty hand reduces the hero to minimal effectiveness
- turns a run-ending moment into a survivable crisis

### Interactions

- Death fork: prevents the fork from triggering at all.
- Iron Last Stand Warden: both prevent death outcomes; in combination the surviving hero has more than 1 card and is still functional.
- Sentinel: mitigation reduces the chance the hero reaches near-death in the first place.

### Best play pattern

Road:
- save for true breakpoints where the hero dying ends the run.

Camp:
- after Physician fires, that hero's next priority is drawing to hand cap via Diamonds or camp.

Boss:
- hold until irreversible failure point, especially phase transitions.

## Alchemist — Manipulation

At camp, one hero may discard any number of cards from their hand and draw the same number.

### Effect profile

- targeted hand-quality improvement without losing card count
- enables surgical removal of dead weight before a boss
- strongest in long chapters where hand composition drifts badly

### Interactions

- Quartermaster Logistician: stacks for double-filter at camp.
- Exile: mitigates awkward hand states caused by over-thinning.
- route commitment Abbey lines: strong synergy with recovery-focused landmark rewards.

### Best play pattern

Road:
- accept worse hands during encounters knowing Alchemist can repair at camp.

Camp:
- refresh the hero whose hand composition is worst for the upcoming boss.

Boss:
- Alchemist fires at camp, not during boss; plan before committing.

## Strategic Interactions

Suit interactions:
- Hearts: signature scaling.
- Diamonds: improves chance Hearts recovery becomes immediately useful.
- Spades: mitigation plus recovery creates compounding survivability.
- Clubs: allows controlled aggression due to stronger fallback.

Campaign engine interactions:
- Death fork: Surgeon directly reduces fork frequency by preventing death outright.
- Camp planning: Alchemist gives the party one targeted hand-repair per camp.

## Best Team Context

Best alongside:
- Sentinel for mitigation-recovery loop
- Quartermaster for reliable heart access
- Oracle for curated discard value timing

Risky alongside:
- ultra-thin decks where recovered cards are low utility
- teams that refuse to plan around camp hand-refresh timing

## Common Mistakes

- triggering Hearts when discard value is poor
- spending Physician on avoidable deaths
- using Alchemist refresh on a hero who would not play again before the boss

## Practical Playbook

1. Track discard quality, not just discard size.
2. Reserve Physician for true breakpoints.
3. Route toward recovery landmarks when party is depleted.
4. At camp, use Alchemist on the hero with the worst hand composition.
5. Enter boss with Surgeon resources unspent unless survival requires otherwise.
