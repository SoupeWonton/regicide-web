# Chapter 1 Encounter Pack

Chapter Theme: First Ascension

Chapter Primary Pressure: Access under attrition (hand quality and draw timing)

Chapter Boss Lesson: Enter the Chapter 1 boss with stable turn ownership, preserved hand quality, and deliberate mitigation timing.

Design note:
- Encounters teach one primary pressure axis.
- Secondary pressure is intentionally light.
- No encounter requires a single class to solve.

## Skirmishes (10)

### 1) Cracked Buckler
Name: Cracked Buckler
CT Category: Shield
Secondary Category: Access
Pressure Rating: 0.25
Narrative Theme: Thin armor breaks if the first exchange is sloppy.
Mechanics: First counterattack each enemy gains +1 unless a Spade was played this round.
Why It Creates Pressure: Punishes delayed mitigation.
Counterplay: Spend a small Spade early, then stabilize.
What Skill It Teaches: Protect the opening turn.
What Chapter Boss Lesson It Reinforces: Early boss mistakes cost cards you cannot easily recover.
