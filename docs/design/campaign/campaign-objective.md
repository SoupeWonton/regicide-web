# Campaign Objective

## v0 Product Intent

Build a replayable Chapter 1 -> Chapter 2 campaign loop that feels like:
- familiar Regicide fundamentals in Chapter 1
- punishing, rewarding escalation in Chapter 2

## Core Feeling

The campaign should always feel close to collapse.

Progression is not raw power fantasy. Progression is better planning, clearer role expression, and harder-earned survival.

## Design Layers

- Regicide layer: turn-by-turn tactical card combat
- Road layer: deterministic route pressure, partial information, reward tradeoffs
- Campaign layer: lineage, death/replacement, Memories, and Kingdom unlock persistence

## v0 Success Criteria

- Players understand the new campaign loop by the end of Chapter 1.
- Players feel Chapter 2 is a major difficulty step.
- Completion of Chapter 2 feels earned and motivates expansion beyond v0.

## Loss Philosophy

Loss should still move long-term progression:
- chapter/class unlock pathways
- persistent Kingdom state
- stronger next-run decision quality

A broken team is not wasted playtime.

## Campaign Unit

The core unit of play is:

Kingdom -> Chapter -> Team -> route commitment -> encounter -> camp/interlude planning -> mandatory pre-boss camp -> boss attempt -> chapter progression.

This structure is the source of tension and replayability for v0.
