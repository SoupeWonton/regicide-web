# Chapters

Chapter roadmap and difficulty intent for v0.

## Scope Lock

v0 includes only:
- Chapter 1
- Chapter 2

Chapter 3+ is deferred until Chapter 2 balance stabilizes.

## Difficulty Framing

Difficulty is judged relative to base Regicide.

- Base Regicide reference is high difficulty.
- Chapter 1 should teach campaign flow without removing tension.
- Chapter 2 should force deeper planning and feel materially harder.

## Chapter 1: First Ascension

Design goal:
- familiar Regicide core with campaign systems layered in

Loop:
- choose landmark path
- resolve encounters
- enter mandatory pre-boss camp/interlude
- fight Chapter 1 boss
- resolve post-boss interlude and rewards

Boss canon:
- literal base Regicide 12-enemy castle sequence
- no Chapter 1 boss modifier

Outcome intent:
- players learn class identity, camp preparation flow, and lineage stakes

## Chapter 2: Broken Court

Design goal:
- hard, high-reward chapter that validates the campaign model

Loop:
- deterministic map variant with partial information
- stronger encounter pressure and stricter reward-routing consequences
- mandatory pre-boss interlude and prep decision
- boss with Chapter 2 difficulty escalation (details intentionally open)

Outcome intent:
- players should need multiple attempts and cleaner planning
- chapter completion should feel earned and unlock confidence for future chapter design

## Player Count Notes

- Existing multiplayer model remains valid for 1-4 players.
- Four players is the preferred full-party reference setup.
- One hero per player remains the canonical control model.

## Unlock Milestones

- Completing Chapter 1 unlocks specialization progression pathways.
- Chapter completion and other progression events can unlock additional classes.
- Chapter unlock persistence is Kingdom-level and permanent.

## Deferred Content

Deferred until post-v0 iteration:
- Chapter 3+
- ascension mode structure
- final Chapter 2 boss modifier pool lock
