## The bar

- **One of four suit identities** (♣ attack · ♦ draw · ♠ block · ♥ recover) — never a second of a suit.
- Tiers: **Fragment** (castable emergency) → **Half** (strongest castable) → **Full** (V3.5, non-castable win token).
- Held in the **gauntlet**; lives in a dedicated area (not shuffled in, not against the hand limit);
  **casting consumes**; at most one of each suit per combat; **sits above matching suit immunity**.
