## The bar

- **Converts the class's key moment into ONE resource that matters** — and only four qualify:
  **cards · library/deck-size · damage · deck-quality (recruits/grafts).**
- **Multiplicative, not additive** — scales with a native quantity you control (shield built, Spade
  value, combo size, discard pile); never small flat bonuses, never "all enemies".
- **Three rungs rising C2 → C4**, each strictly stronger than the last. **A rung never restates a passive.**
- One ladder per suit axis (♠ survival · ♥ recycle · ♦ cards · ♣ damage).
