## F. Foundational — versioned card-state model *(build FIRST; blocks §1, §7-equip, Sanctum, UI)*

The live game keys cards by **logical suit/rank**. Replacement grafts, Sanctum transfers, and the UI's
printed-vs-effective display need a richer model that does **not** exist yet:
- 🏗️ **Stable physical card ID** (identity survives rank/suit replacement).
- 🏗️ **Original rank + suit** and **effective rank + suit** (printed vs. grafted).
- 🏗️ **Graft provenance** — what was replaced, by what, and the ability to **move a graft** (Sanctum)
  without losing the card underneath.
- 🏗️ **State serialization + schema versioning** (persisted run/lineage format changes).

Until §F lands, §1 (grafts), §6 (relic/graft effects), Sanctum, replay/debug, and UI projections are
fragile. **This is the first vertical slice.**

# V3.0 system specs (decided design, no code)

## 1. Graft — additive → replacement
*Decided [`2026-06-20-replacement-graft-semantics`](../../decisions/2026-06-20-replacement-graft-semantics.md); extended 2026-06-25; trigger/cap clarified [`2026-06-27` Decision 3](../../decisions/2026-06-27-v3.0-question-sweep.md).*

- **The trigger is ownership, not continent.** An exact kill of a card **not yet owned → recruit**;
  an exact kill of an **already-recruited** card → **graft**. The graft **replaces the suit OR the
  value (rank)** of one held card — **never `+1`, never an added second suit** (old `hone`=+1 /
  `graft`=add-suit are both replaced).
- **Grafts therefore fire in C1 too** — whenever you exact-kill a duplicate of a card you already
  recruited. **From C2 onward every ordinary road exact-kill is a graft** simply because the number
  deck is complete (every card is owned), which keeps post-recruitment roads alive.
- **Royal grafts cap value at 10** (§3).
- **Open (Q28/§Q):** whether a redundant exact kill (the graft trigger) *also* rolls the **agnostic
  50/50 fragment** drop, or the two are separated. *(Do not assume live behavior here — audit `91d3677`.)*
- **Graft rearranging is now the Sanctum** landmark (Forge = spells-only — §8). Its verb is
  **resolved** (Rearrange = relocate an earned graft; §8 / Q33); only swap-vs-single and budget remain
  as tuning.
- **⚠ Build delta — VERIFY AGAINST LIVE FIRST.** Earlier notes assumed `reforge`/`transmute` tokens and
  an `EXPERIMENTS.replacementGrafts` flag **already exist** on the live branch and only need enabling.
  **An audit found this is not safe to assume — treat replacement grafting as to-be-built, not enabled.**
  The **first slice is a card-state audit** (see "Foundational architecture" below): replacement grafts
  need **stable physical card IDs + original-vs-effective rank/suit + graft provenance**, which the live
  card model (keyed by logical suit/rank) does not provide. Only after that model exists: route owned-card
  exact kills to suit-or-value replacement, **retire the additive `hone` (+1) / `graft` (add-suit) path**,
  and update UI/tests + the persisted run/lineage state format. Still the highest-leverage delta — but it
  is **build**, not a flag-flip.

## 2. Classes — home-suit **path** + a separate selectable **Staff**
*Decided [`2026-06-24` Decision C](../../decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment.md); **re-clarified [`2026-06-27` Decision 1](../../decisions/2026-06-27-v3.0-question-sweep.md)** (path and Staff decoupled — supersedes the earlier "Staff = home suit / Tree of 3" framing). Path content: [`facet-and-linear-candidates`](../../wiki/v3/classes/facet-and-linear-candidates.md).*

- **Path and Staff are decoupled.** Each class has **four suit paths** (♠/♥/♦/♣ ladders) and **four
  passive-signature Staffs**; on the class-select screen you **pick one of your class's four Staffs**.
- **A class starts on its home-suit path:** Sentinel ♠, Executioner ♣, Quartermaster ♦, Surgeon ♥.
- **In V3.0 only the path's C2 rung is live**, and it is a **single ability — the ladder's first
  rung**: entering C2 grants it (revealed by an animation *(Gab: how)*) and that's all; it does **not**
  rung up within C2. **Completing C2 unlocks all three other paths**, open and selectable on entering
  C2 *(supersedes the earlier "one player-chosen tree")*. C3/C4/C5 rungs ship **visible-but-locked**.
- **The matrix is 4 classes × 4 suit paths = 16 ladders** (all drafted this session — including the
  **♠ column Bastion / Bloodward / Rationing / Convalescence**), with the **Staff as an orthogonal
  selectable layer** (no Staff "diagonal").
- **The live siege ultimate (`siegeText`) is retired entirely.** The existing passive (`abilityText`)
  becomes one of the **selectable Staffs**; **Staff swapping is the Fallen Heroes shrine** (unlocked
  after C1; the swap offers **one randomly-drawn Staff from each of the four classes** — §8/§10).
- **C2 rung resolved (2026-06-27):** it is a **single ability** (the ladder's first rung), not a
  within-C2 ladder. **Staff roster resolved (2026-06-28):** the Staffs **are the four passive signatures
  per class** already authored in [`facet-and-linear-candidates`](../../wiki/v3/classes/facet-and-linear-candidates.md)
  (16 total); at class-select you pick **one of your class's four**. ⚠ The **roster** is set, but several
  **passive/path ability semantics are still incomplete or reference rules that don't exist** — each must
  be pinned before implementation (§Q #4), not merely "numeric tuning."

## 3. C2 royal gates — the 3 / 2 / 1 pyramid + crown
*Decided [`2026-06-25-acquisition-cadence-and-royal-gates` Decision B](../../decisions/2026-06-25-acquisition-cadence-and-royal-gates.md) — **the 3/2/1 pyramid is Gab-approved (2026-06-27).***

- C2 contains three gates, each a narrowing identity decision **made after the gate fight**:
  - **Jack Gate** — *"which Jack do you leave behind?"* keep **3 of 4**.
  - **Queen Gate** — *"which two Queens follow you?"* keep **2 of 4**.
  - **King Gate** — *"which crown do you wear?"* keep **1 of 4**; the betrayer-King abdicates.
- All six recruited royals (3J + 2Q + 1K) are **real deck cards**, shuffled in. C3-entry deck ≈ **46
  cards** (40 numbers + 6 royals). Royals you leave behind are **still fought** at the gate; you just
  don't recruit them.
- **Specialization does not live in the King** — build identity rides on the suit ladder/crystal; the
  crown is a flavor capstone, **fixed through C3**.
- **V3.0 victory = completing C2** (defeat the first King at the King Gate).
- **Royal kills graft (capped at 10)** *(resolved [`2026-06-27` Decision 3](../../decisions/2026-06-27-v3.0-question-sweep.md))* — exact-killing any J/Q/K at a gate triggers a graft whose value is
  capped at 10, regardless of the 3/2/1 keep choice (settles the old skipped-royal question).
- **Open:** validate the 46-card deck cycles ♦/♥ adequately (sim). C4 re-crown deferred to V3.5.

## 4. Acquisition funnel — closing funnel that ends at the C2 seam
*Decided [`2026-06-25` Decision A](../../decisions/2026-06-25-acquisition-cadence-and-royal-gates.md).*

| Continent | Beat | Verb | Acquisition |
|---|---|---|---|
| C1 | Claim | **Recruit** | Exact-kill recruits numbers **6–10**; **Hunt lives here and only here**; deck reaches full A–10. |
| C2 | Shape | **Royal gates** | Road exact-kills are **grafts**; royals acquired at the three gates. |

Acquisition **closes at the end of C2**; from C3 (V3.5) power climbs through *depth* on a fixed pool.

## 5. Forgiveness — front-loaded
*Decided [`2026-06-25` Decision C](../../decisions/2026-06-25-acquisition-cadence-and-royal-gates.md) + [`2026-06-27` Decision 8](../../decisions/2026-06-27-v3.0-question-sweep.md).*

- **Opening guarantee (resolved):** the opening hand **always contains at least one Diamond** — no run
  can begin unable to draw.
- **Road recovery = Camp, a fixed three-part rest (resolved 2026-06-27):** reshuffle discard → Tavern,
  redraw a fresh hand to **max hand size 5**, and **first attack deals double**. The whole bundle fires
  (not a pick-one menu). (Don't make Camp mandatory.)
- Philosophy: **forgiveness front-loaded into openings/seams; difficulty back-loaded into the royal
  gates.** The deck can lose a fight; it cannot soft-lock.
- **Open (numbers, need playtest):** Camp magnitudes (the redraw-to-5 / double-first-attack are the
  shape; values still tune by play). Note the **placement** is resolved — a rest falls at **every
  province seam** (Decision 11). **One thing to settle:** is that province-seam rest the **Camp**
  landmark itself, or a separate automatic reset? (the live code already has a mandatory pre-boss
  camp.) (Q16)

## 6. Spells — crystals (Fragment + Half ship in V3.0)
*Decided [`2026-06-24` Decision A](../../decisions/2026-06-24-crystals-continents-and-equipment.md); Fragment+Half confirmed for V3.0 by [`2026-06-25` Decision A](../../decisions/2026-06-25-v3-scope-c1-c2.md). Per-suit text: [`spells/proposed-design`](../../wiki/v3/spells/proposed-design/).*

- **Four spell identities, one per suit**, never a second of a suit. Held in the **gauntlet** (name
  confirmed), which carries exactly four. Live in a **dedicated area** (not shuffled into the Tavern,
  not against the hand limit); **casting consumes**; in-combat cap is one castable per suit.
- **Fragments & tiers (resolved 2026-06-28, [decision](../../decisions/2026-06-28-relic-slots-fragments-and-ui.md)):**
  **fragments are agnostic** (generic, not suit-typed) and **drop 50/50 after each encounter**. The
  **bracelet** is the **between-encounter UI** where the player places fragments into the gauntlet's
  suit holes (♠♦♥♣) and previews the next encounter's spell ([UI: `ui-ux-v3.0`](ui-ux-v3.0.md)). **Tiers
  coexist; fragments accumulate** — inputting one is the same action at every tier: **equip** to use a
  suit's spell now, or **sandbag** to build it up. **Fragment** (castable) → **Half** (strongest
  castable) → **Full** (V3.5 win token, non-castable). *(Revises "suit-specific fragments pooled by
  encounter count.")*
- **Forge (resolved 2026-06-28):** verb = **forge** (retained) — the **bracelet** places/equips/sandbags

## 10. Meta / lineage / death
*Decided [`2026-06-25` Decisions B, D, F](../../decisions/2026-06-25-v3-scope-c1-c2.md); unlock breadth revised [`2026-06-27` Decision 1](../../decisions/2026-06-27-v3.0-question-sweep.md).*
